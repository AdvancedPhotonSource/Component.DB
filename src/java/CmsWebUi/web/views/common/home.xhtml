A major effort in the conceptual design phase of the MBA accelerator is identifying, defining and tracking component types and component lists necessary to build the new facility. Reasonable estimates of cost and labor will depend on the thoroughness and accuracy of these lists. The primary goal of the Component Management System is to capture required components and assist in defining the conceptual design of the new accelerator.
